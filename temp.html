Our north star

Craft meets performance.

SoftSheep sits at the intersection of design, engineering, and longevity. The goal is simple: build tools that feel native on day one and still trustworthy years later.

Native finesse

SwiftUI, AppKit, and system frameworks used the way they were meant to be used. Every interaction leans on macOS conventions so nothing feels bolted on.

Considered minimalism

Restraint as a feature. Typography leads, color supports, and animation earns its spot by clarifying what’s happening on screen.

Built to outlast trends

Composable architecture, testable modules, and automation-friendly tooling make it easier to keep apps healthy as macOS evolves.

Meet the founder

Tiberon Kuiper

Tiberon works at the intersection of medicine and software, with a focus on macOS-first tools and privacy-respecting AI. SoftSheep is the umbrella for projects that need more care and polish than a quick side repo.

  • Building macOS apps with Swift and SwiftUI, heavily inspired by Apple and MacPaw
  • Active in healthcare-focused AI and clinical tooling, including work with LUMC
  • Based in the Netherlands, collaborating remotely when it makes sense
